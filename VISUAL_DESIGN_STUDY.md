## ğŸ“ **Typography and Hierarchy**

**Font Selection:**  
Inter is the primary font for all UI elements. Chosen for its readability and balanced character proportions, Inter conveys clarity without unnecessary embellishment.

**Hierarchy:**  
Hierarchy is established primarily through font weight (bold for headings, regular for body text, medium for labels) rather than large variations in size. This creates a more ordered and less visually cluttered interface.

**Grid System:**  
Spacing, margins, and font sizes are based on an 8px grid, which lends a consistent rhythm and structure across the application.

## ğŸ“ **Spacing & Layout**

**White Space:**  
Generous use of white space is key to the visual approach. Components and controls are surrounded by ample margins and padding, making the interface feel open, approachable, and easy to navigate.

**8px Grid Alignment:**  
All layout and spacing decisions are made in multiples of 8 pixels, creating order and visual alignment throughout the design.

**Simplicity:**  
The number of buttons and controls is deliberately minimized. Only essential actions are presented, reducing cognitive overhead and making the interface feel streamlined and accessible.
